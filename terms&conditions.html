Placement terms and conditions :

The sole purpose of this training at INDIAN ADVANCED CLINICAL RESEARCH is to help and guide achieving a successful Career to students. And with this aim we stand with the students till they achieve this objective.

Placement assistance will be genuinely provided to each of the candidates and then the Placements will be not being much of a challenge to those who excels in training, presentation Skills and positive attitude.

INDIAN ADVANCED CLINICAL RESEARCH believes in promise less and deliver more IDEALOGY and hence will strive in this direction to fulfil the aspirations of the students.

No employment or recruitment is guaranteed by Indian Advanced Clinical Research/its associates or placement assurance for job will not be provided to influence in completion of these courses.

Job opening details will be duly shared with the students as in when it is available within the industry and to our knowledge.

Students will be helped with the job opportunities till the time one doesn’t get placed.

Selection of the candidate by the company is solely based on the candidate’s skills and Interview performance.

Placement Rules for the students:

Every student shall be eligible to participate in the Placement process provided he/she has completed all parameters to be eligible for placements.

He/she has completed the payment dues.

Within the time frame of interview process (from 2nd Month till the 6th Month from the date of Joining) and receiving a job offer a student can appear for as many interviews as held by the institute.

Based on the eligibility criteria provided by the company’s job information, details will be shared with the students and the shortlisting process is entirely the desecration of the future employers.

Encourage students to be familiar with the Healthcare job portals for the additional source of job alerts.

Disqualification from Placement Activity: The Institute expects a high level of moral, ethical and professional behavior from all its students. Therefore, in the interest of the students and Institute, any student can be disqualified from the placement opportunity and appropriate actions can be initiated against him/her if his/her behavior is not found befitting the institute.

Placement Rules:
  1. Pan India Opportunities: All placement opportunities are available across India, not only in one state or geographical region.
  2. Relocation Possibility: Candidates may be needed to move depending on the job availability and prospects given.
  3. No Cap on CTC/Job Profiles/Location: There is no limit to the available CTC, work profiles, or job locations; options are diversified and endless.
  4. Performance-based Selection: The selection and CTC offered are determined by individual performance throughout the selection process.
  5. Placement Assurance Conditions: Placement assurance is only valid to students receiving free training through campus drives if all of the Institute’s terms and conditions are followed.
Pre placement talks:
  1. Role of the Placement Team: The Placement Team’s major responsibility is to facilitate placement-related activities.
  2. Recruitment Program Eligibility: Eligibility requirements include 80% attendance at all technical and skill development courses, timely completion of assignments, and participation in relevant sessions.
  3. Consultation before Registration: Candidates are urged to contact the Placement Team with any questions before registering for any industry.
  4. Communication with Placement Team: All candidate inquiries and grievances will be handled by the placement team.
Placement process:
  1. Basic Preparation for Interviews: Lack of basic interview preparation may lead to debarment for a specified period.
  2. Commitment to Interview Processes: Once registered, candidates cannot drop from further interview processes.
  3. Performance in Subsequent Processes: Shortlisted candidates must maintain performance standards in subsequent selection processes.
  4. Placement Opportunities Criteria: All placement options are based on individual course performance, individual ratings, academic qualifications, academic scores, previous experiences, and so forth.
Attendance & Punctuality:
  1. Mandatory Training Programs: Candidates interested in campus placement must attend various training programs organized by the Placement Team.
Discipline:
  1. Disqualification for Non-attendance: Failure to attend an interview without prior permission will result in permanent disqualification from placement activities.
  2. Decorum and Professionalism: Candidates must uphold decorum and professionalism during the entire selection process.
  3. Non-negotiable Details: Negotiations with the industry over CTC, location, and other issues are not authorised unless specified in the registration communication.
  4. Joining Company Obligation: Once selected, learners are obligated to join the company on the specified date.
  5. Disciplinary Action for Violation: Violation of any rule will result in disciplinary action.

Further Chances with Permission: Once selected, students may be given more opportunities with prior authorization from the Placement Team on a case-by-case basis.

Note :

INDIAN ADVANCED CLINICAL RESEARCH keeps its rights reserved to make changes in the Placement Policy any time as may be appropriate. The placement rules as may be modified from time to time will be binding to all students.
