Refund Policy

At IACR, we are committed to providing high-quality education and training programs to our students. We understand that circumstances may arise that require a refund of tuition fees. This refund policy outlines the terms and conditions governing refund requests for our programs.

We refund the Full amount before five days of commencement of the class. If five days have gone by since you’re buying a course, unfortunately, we can’t offer you a refund.

To complete your refund, we require you to send a formal email with a valid reason for canceling your enrollment with Payment Acknowledgement.

Refund and Transfer

 Under no circumstances can the applicant’s course enrollment costs be refunded or transferred after a week of class commencement. IACR will neither accept or initiate requests for fee transfers or refunds after 5 days of class commencement. IACR has a strict no refund policy.

Once the admission fee is paid, it is non-refundable under any circumstances. IACR follows a rigorous no-refund policy. Money sent to IACR for educational purposes cannot be returned to the student under any circumstances.

Maintaining Decorum and Discipline

Applicants are expected to maintain decorum and discipline during the course. LLRI reserves the right to cancel enrollment without compensation or refund in the case of a breach of contract by the applicant.
